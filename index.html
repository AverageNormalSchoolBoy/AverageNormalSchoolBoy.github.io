About Me

I am a PhD student in mathematics at [University Name], advised by Prof. [Advisor]. My research focuses on lattice theory, category theory, and their applications in algebra and topology.

I study structures such as distributive lattices, posets, and their morphisms. For example, one problem I consider is: $$ \text{When does } \bigvee_{i \in I} f(x_i) = f\left(\bigvee_{i \in I} x_i\right) \text{ hold?} $$

  \node (M0) [right of=L0] {$0_M$};
  \node (M1) [right of=L1] {$b$};
  \node (M2) [right of=L2] {$1_M$};

  \draw (L0) -- (L1) -- (L2);
  \draw (M0) -- (M1) -- (M2);
  \draw[dashed] (L0) -- (M0);
  \draw[dashed] (L1) -- (M1);
  \draw[dashed] (L2) -- (M2);
\end{tikzpicture>
