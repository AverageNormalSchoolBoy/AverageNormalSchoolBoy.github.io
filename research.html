Research

I am a PhD student in mathematics interested in lattice theory, category theory, and the algebraic structures that arise in combinatorial representation theory. My current work explores how morphisms between finite lattices reflect structural symmetries and topological properties.

Current Projects

  • Canonical morphisms in modular lattices. Studying the hom-structure between distributive and modular lattices with attention to categorical dualities.
  • Quiver diagrams and order-preserving maps. An exploration of how directed graphs encode morphisms of partially ordered sets and their representations.

Mathematical Interests

Broadly speaking, I’m interested in the algebraic and categorical underpinnings of order structures. Specific topics include:

  • Finite lattices and congruence relations
  • Quiver diagrams and functorial correspondences
  • Homological methods in combinatorics
  • Connections to representation theory and topology
